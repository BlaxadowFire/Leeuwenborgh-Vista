You can't use the mouse in the game.

If you accidentally used your mouse, just press enter to fix it.

You can press escape any time to open an in game menu.

Use the numbers 0-9 to navigate in the game.

If the game looks a bit awkward or odd, you can refresh the current page by pressing enter.

Pressing escape will mostly everytime bring you to an in game menu.

If you are stuck in a puzzle, pressing escape twice will bing you back to the room.

Tip: One of the most used tricks is usage of capital letters. Make sure you pay close attention.
